MoneyHolder

Moneyholder is a desktop application, thanks to which you will no longer have to worry about your funds. This app will show all your incomes and outcomes in simple and easy to read form of charts and tables,it will automatically fill the date and calculate how much have you spent, earned and what is the current balance of your funds Windows english version Windows polish version
